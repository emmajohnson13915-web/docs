## What is JSONViewer AI?

JSONViewer AI helps you make sense of complex JSON data without any technical knowledge. Simply paste your JSON data, and our AI instantly transforms it into easy-to-read formats, explanations, and visualizations.

## How to use JSONViewer AI

<Steps>
  <Step title="Paste your JSON data">
    Copy the JSON data you want to understand and paste it into the viewer. You can get JSON data from APIs, files, or any other source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose what you need">
    Select from different views:
    - **Pretty view** - See your data organized and color-coded
    - **Table view** - View data in rows and columns
    - **AI explanation** - Get a plain-English summary of what the data means
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore and understand">
    Click on any part of your data to expand or collapse sections. Use the AI chat to ask questions about your data in plain language.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for getting started

<Tip>
  **Start small** - Try pasting a simple JSON example first to get familiar with the interface before working with complex data.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Use the AI chat** - Don't hesitate to ask questions like "What does this data represent?" or "Show me all the email addresses in this data."
</Tip>

<Warning>
  **Sensitive data** - Be cautious when pasting sensitive information. Use our privacy mode for confidential data.
</Warning>
